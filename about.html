My journey began in the world of science, where I discovered my love for chemistry and the joy of helping others understand complex concepts. As I delved deeper into the world of technology, I found a new passion in coding. I’ve honed my skills in frontend development, creating intuitive and engaging user interfaces, while also mastering backend development to ensure seamless functionality and performance. I believe in the power of education and technology to transform lives. My goal is to bridge the gap between these two fields, creating innovative solutions that enhance learning experiences. Whether I’m tutoring a student in chemistry or developing a web application, I strive to inspire curiosity and a love for learning. When I’m not teaching or coding, you can find me exploring the latest tech trends, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, or enjoying the great outdoors. I’m always eager to connect with fellow educators, developers, and anyone who shares a passion for science and technology. Let’s collaborate and create something amazing together!
